# 5.6.2. Cleaning up Amazon DynamoDB

1. Navigate to **Amazon DynamoDB** -> **Tables**.
2. Select all workshop tables (`MatchmakingQueue` and `ActiveMatches`), click **Delete**, and confirm deletion of all tables.
